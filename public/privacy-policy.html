Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Softlet Web Highlights ("the Extension") is a personal productivity tool that allows users to highlight text on webpages and save those highlights for later reference.

Data We Collect

When you use the Extension, the following data is stored in your personal account:

  • Highlighted text — the text you select and highlight on webpages.
  • Page metadata — the URL, page title, and favicon of pages where you create highlights.
  • Highlight metadata — the color you chose and the position of the highlight on the page.
  • Authentication data — your Google account email and name, used solely for sign-in.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide the highlighting and sync functionality of the Extension. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties.

Data Storage

All data is stored securely in a Supabase-hosted PostgreSQL database with row-level security, ensuring that only you can access your own highlights. Authentication tokens are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API.

Data Deletion

You can delete individual highlights or entire pages of highlights at any time from the Extension or the Softlet Tools dashboard. Deleted data is permanently removed from our database.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database hosting and authentication (supabase.com/privacy)
  • Google OAuth — sign-in only, we do not access any other Google data
